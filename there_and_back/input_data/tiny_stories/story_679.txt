Once upon a time, there was a fox. The fox liked to play in the deep woods. He had many friends who played with him.
One day, the fox saw something new. It was a toy. The toy could spin. The fox wanted to show his friends the toy that could spin.
The fox and his friends played with the spinning toy all day. They took turns making it spin. They were so happy. The fox loved his deep woods and his spinning toy.
