Once upon a time, there was a boy named Tim. Tim loved to watch movies. His favorite movie was about a big race. In the race, many cars tried to win. Tim wanted to win a race too.
One day, Tim saw a helpless little dog. The dog was sad and lost. Tim wanted to help the dog. He took the dog home and gave it food and love. The dog was happy and became Tim's best friend.
Tim and his dog, Spot, joined a race together. They ran very fast. Tim and Spot helped each other and never gave up. At the end of the race, Tim and Spot won. They were very happy. The moral of the story is: when you help others, you can win together.
