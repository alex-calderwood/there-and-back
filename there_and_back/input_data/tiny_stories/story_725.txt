Once upon a time, in a gray house, there lived a gray figure. This figure was a cat. The cat liked to play with his friends. They were all happy.
One day, the gray cat had an idea. He wanted to unite all his friends for a big party. The gray cat told his friends about the party. They were all excited.
At the party, something unexpected happened. A big dog came to the party. The friends were scared. But, the big dog was not mean. He wanted to be friends too. The gray cat and his friends let the big dog join the party. They all had fun and were united.
