Once upon a time, there was a human. The human was fearful. He wanted to unlock a big door. He was scared of what was behind the door, but he knew he had to open it.
One day, he found the key. He put the key in the door and turned it. The door opened. He was ready to see something scary. But, instead, he saw a big, happy dog. The dog jumped and licked his face. The human was not scared anymore.
The human and the dog became best friends. They played and laughed together. The human was not fearful anymore. The big door had a nice surprise. The human was happy he unlocked it.
