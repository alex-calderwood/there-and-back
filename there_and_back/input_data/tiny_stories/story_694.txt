Sam liked to play baseball with his dad. He wanted to improve his hitting and catching. He practiced every day in the backyard. He threw the ball high and low, and hit it with his bat. He ran after the ball and caught it with his glove.
One day, his dad surprised him with a fancy new bat. It was shiny and red, and had his name on it. Sam was very happy and thanked his dad. He wanted to try his new bat right away. He asked his dad to pitch him the ball.
His dad threw the ball to Sam. Sam swung his new bat and hit the ball hard. The ball flew over the fence and into the street. Sam and his dad cheered and hugged. Sam had improved his hitting with his fancy new bat. He felt proud and excited. He loved to play baseball with his dad.
